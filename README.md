Customer Churn: Prediction & Retention Strategy
Project Documentation

1. Executive Summary
In an era where customer retention is as critical as acquisition, this project addresses the challenge of churn within the telecommunications industry. By deploying an advanced Random Forest predictive model, we have transitioned from reactive service management to proactive customer engagement. This report details the end-to-end analytical pipeline, from exploratory data analysis to the deployment of actionable business insights, demonstrating a clear focus on enhancing customer lifetime value and reducing attrition rates.

3. Strategic Recommendations
Contract Conversion: Shift month-to-month contract users to long-term plans through targeted loyalty discounts.

Early Intervention: Target customers identified by the model with high churn probability (0.3+) for personalized retention offers.

Tenure-Based Rewards: Enhance rewards for long-term customers to increase their Lifetime Value (LTV).

4. Conclusion & Strategic Impact
The findings of this analysis underscore that customer churn is not an unavoidable cost of doing business, but a manageable variable. By identifying high-risk segments—particularly those under month-to-month contracts—and prioritizing them through our predictive threshold tuning (Recall 77%), the organization is well-positioned to optimize its retention budget. Moving forward, the integration of these insights into daily CRM workflows will be essential to sustain long-term growth and strengthen customer loyalty.
